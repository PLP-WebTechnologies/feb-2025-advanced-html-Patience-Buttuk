Le petit gateau baking academy

Hello, welcome to Lepetitgateauke baking classes. we offer both beginner and professional classes. We hope you find something that interests you.



Beginner course outline

  1. Introduction to baking
  2. Basic cakes and cupcakes
  3. Cookies and biscuits
  4. Bread making basics
  5. Pastries and tarts
  6. Frostings and fillings

Advanced course outline

  1. Advanced cake baking and decoration
  2. Artisan bread and sourdough mastery
  3. French pastry techniques
  4. Chocolate and confectionary
  5. Wedding and celebration cakes
  6. Gluten free and alternative baking
